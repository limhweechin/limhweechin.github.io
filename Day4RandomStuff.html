CGSS Talent Development

​The mission of the CGSS Talent Development Programme is to Nurture Talents in Leaders of Character , which is in line with the school’s mission to Nurture Leaders of Character. The goals of the Talent Development Programme are to enable the talents of Cedar to:

  • stretch their academic potential, through the Academies of Mathematics & Sciences, as well as English Language and Humanities.
  • develop the skills & dispositions of a practitioner in the discipline.
  • have an enriched learning experience of how practitioners engage in their disciplines to achieve high levels of accomplishments.
  • be respected as aspiring leaders

In tandem with our school’s mission, vision and values, the talents of Cedar will achieve the following objectives at the end of the programme:

  • be equipped with 21st century competencies, such as critical thinking skills, creative thinking skills, and problem-solving skills for active citizenry in a globalized world.
  • able to create and develop authentic products or services to bring about a desired impact on mankind and the community.
  • be highly sought-after by the top Junior Colleges, gaining admission into the junior college and faculty of their choice.
  • have a competitive edge over others in applications for scholarships and awards.
  • be empathetic towards the needs of the society and contribute towards helping the less privileged with humility and integrity.
  • be equipped with leadership skills to develop their leadership talents through various training programmes.
The CEDAR Approach to Talent Development
Connect

Connect with like-minded peers and find out more about how you can further develop your thinking and feeling processes together through our TD enhancement activities.

Enrich

Laboratory tour at A*Star for science enthusiasts, SUTD Music Maker workshop to appeal to the musically inclined and  Design Thinking workshop for future architects -- embark on an adventure to fuel your scientific interests!

Deepen

The best way to learn is by doing. Deepen your understanding of your chosen field by engaging in a research project. 

Achieve

Go beyond your comfort zone. Pit your knowledge, skills and ideas against the best and brightest. Learn and improve with every step. Participate in competitions to challenge yourself.

Refine

All of us need a little polishing. To help you put your best foot forward, we have lined up a series of workshops that can help better prepare you for the journey beyond Cedar.
